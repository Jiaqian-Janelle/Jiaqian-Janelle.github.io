## Core idea

Incorrect traces preserve exploratory behavior near the model's capability boundary, while
correct traces tend to be shorter and less reflective. ReNIO uses the student/teacher prefix
probability ratio to find pivotal tokens leading to wrong reasoning and weights those
trajectories more — keeping OPD's prefix-training advantage (no full rollouts needed).
