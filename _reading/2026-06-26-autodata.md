## Core idea

Agentic Self-Instruct: an inner loop where agents build datasets by sampling, reasoning, and
self-feedback; an outer meta-optimizer improves the agent's data-generation policy over time.
Turns extra inference compute into higher training-data quality; gains on CS research, legal
reasoning, and math vs. classical synthetic-data methods.
